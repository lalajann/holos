Holos project explores applicability of Benford’s law in various spheres of human activity and natural processes, revealing the beauty of harmonic mathematical world order. Each digit corresponds to a specific musical note that in combination creates unique sound of real time data. Random numbers used in data generating processes are not distributed identically, therefore it is almost impossible to replicate this natural digits distribution. Try to create data visualization by entering random numbers Yourself. One can also check the approximate validity of data by comparing it to Benford’s distribution.
Benford’s Law is the phenomenon that predicts distribution of leading digits in many real-life sets of numerical data. The graph shows percentage value of digits distribution. It is intriguing that this unique law can be applied to various datasets, unveiling the mysterious process of universal creation.
